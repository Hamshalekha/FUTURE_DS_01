# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Future Interns - Data Science & Analytics Internship (Task 3)

### Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using the Bank Marketing Dataset. The objective is to identify conversion trends, evaluate marketing effectiveness, and provide recommendations to improve customer acquisition and campaign success rates.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer conversion patterns.
* Evaluate marketing channel performance.
* Identify factors influencing conversions.
* Discover high-performing customer segments.
* Provide data-driven recommendations to improve campaign outcomes.

---

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Jupyter Notebook
* Tableau Public

---

## Dataset Information

Dataset: Bank Marketing Dataset (UCI Repository)

Records: 41,188

Features: 21

Target Variable:

* y = yes (Converted)

## Key Findings

### Contact Method

Cellular campaigns significantly outperform telephone campaigns.

### Customer Segments

Students and retired customers demonstrate the strongest conversion performance.

### Previous Campaign Success

Customers who previously converted show the highest likelihood of future conversion.

### Seasonal Trends

March and December recorded the highest conversion rates.

---

## Recommendations

1. Increase focus on cellular marketing campaigns.
2. Retarget customers with successful campaign history.
3. Prioritize high-converting customer segments.
4. Increase marketing efforts during peak conversion months.
5. Use customer segmentation for personalized campaigns.
